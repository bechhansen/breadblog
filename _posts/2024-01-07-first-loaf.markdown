### Specs
- Hydration ~72%
- Recipe from https://www.theperfectloaf.com/best-sourdough-recipe/

### Levain
- 18g Ripe sourdough starter 
- 18g Wheat flour
- 18g Rye flour
- 36g water

### Autolyse 
- 493g Wheat Manitoba - Valsemøllen
- 38g Spelt flour - Skærtoft Mølle
- 232g water + some

### Mix
- 47g water
- 10g salt
- 90g sourdough
