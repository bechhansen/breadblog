### Specs
- Hydration ~75%
- Recipe from https://www.theperfectloaf.com/best-sourdough-recipe/

### Levain
- 21g Ripe sourdough starter 
- 21g Wheat flour
- 21g Rye flour
- 42g water

### Autolyse 
- 575g Wheat flour Tipo 00 - Valsemøllen
- 45g Spelt flour - Skærtoft Mølle
- 386g water

### Mix
- 66g water
- 12g salt
- 105g sourdough
